Easy to use and free to download

Keep your restaurant menu digital and up-to-date with the latest changes.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Scan QR code at the restaurant

2. Guests order food through the app

3. Servers receive the order through the tablet

4. Guests receive their dish, contact free and efficiently

5.Guests pay for their meal through the app

SHOWCASE YOUR MENU

Highlight your menu on a digital platform in a new and easy to access way. Offer recommendations and show your guests the most popular dishes from previous visits.

GET TO KNOW YOUR GUESTS

Through Quickit you can get customer data to personalise the guest experience for when they return. Use the data to save their previous meals and recommend dishes based on what they ordered. For guests with specific dietry requirements, the app saves the data for a more tailored and safe experience within the restaurant.

UPSALE YOUR MENU

Offer your guests upgrades on their orders through the Quickit app. Show your guests that a larger drink or an extra dip is available with their dish.
